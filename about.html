What is FunDom? Why FunDom?

FunDom is a dynamic web application designed for educational purposes, utilizing interactive chat features to foster talent and skill development, personal growth, and effective communication. Learning and engagement are enhanced through community or school-based organization, depending on the course structure.

Our Story

It was founded by Group 2 from BSIT 3B of Colegio de Montalban AY 2024-2025 lead by Shane Lyka T. Tagle, developed by Aron James Betinol, together with Kurt Mellina, Mark Justine Parra, John Kenneth Sangco, Karl Dave Acosta and the rest of the team; Janrow David Gallogo, Jennelyn Serante, Dieza Mae Mostero.

It was originally a chat interaction for students and educators at Colegio de Montalban and look forward to be expanded in other universities until developers reached a 99.9% of the system implementation.

Main Objectives

This Web Application aims to showcase skills of many students, who has incredible talents that can inspired many people. It's about sharing knowledge, improving their passion and discover things. It is a selective app for school community and organization, it only rely inside school.
